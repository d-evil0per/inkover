Why teams use it

Made for people who present and explain for a living.

Does it work across multiple monitors?

Yes. InkOver maintains one transparent overlay per display and keeps them synchronized as monitors change.

Can I keep proof of a session?

Yes. Save snapshots, export PNG or SVG, and record the current display to WebM or GIF.

Can I distribute it professionally?

Yes. The repo includes native build targets and CI release packaging for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
